Reusable Live Event Games

Vi-Activities

Choose an activity, display its QR code, let participants join, and press Start when everyone is ready.

01

Find the Differences

Participants scan the QR, wait for the host start, tap five visual differences, and submit.

 02

Magic Matrix

Participants scan the QR, wait for the host start, and fill the 3x3 square so all rows, columns, and diagonals match the target sum.

 03

Fake or Real

Participants swipe right for Real and left for Fake. Highest correct score wins, with time as the tie-breaker.

 04

Live Leaderboards

Show final rankings for all activities and identify the top 3 winners.
