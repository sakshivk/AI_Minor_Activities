Interactive Ceremony Activities

Welcome to the Minor in AI Graduation Ceremony

Choose one activity, display its QR code, let students join, and press Start when everyone is ready.

01

Find the Differences

Students scan the QR, wait for the host start, tap five visual differences, and submit.

 02

Magic Matrix

Students scan the QR, wait for the host start, and fill the 3x3 square so all rows, columns, and diagonals match the target sum.

 03

Fake or Real

Students swipe right for Real and left for Fake. Highest correct score wins, with time as the tie-breaker.
